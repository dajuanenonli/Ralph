What you need to know

Where can I get Edge Animate?

Edge Animate is only available through the Adobe Creative Cloud.

Already a Creative Cloud Member?

In case you are already a member of the Creative Cloud, you can simply install Edge Animate through the Creative Cloud Updater. Another option is to download the tool on the website: https://creative.adobe.com/products/animate.

Do I have to subscribe to the Creative Cloud?

You don't have to make a full subscription to the Creative Cloud right away. Adobe grants a 30 Day Free Trial, so that you can install the app and check if the tool is working for you. However, if you want to have permanent access to the tools and services, you have to subscribe to the Creative Cloud. You can start your free trial by registering on the product page: https://creative.adobe.com/products/animate.

Download and Installation

Once you have registered for the free trial or signed in with your Creative Cloud ID you can download the tool and follow the instructions for installing Edge Animate on your system. Make sure to check the system requirements first to check if Edge Animate runs on your system:
