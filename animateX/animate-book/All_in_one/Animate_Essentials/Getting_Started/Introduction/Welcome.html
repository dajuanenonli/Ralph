Welcome to this guide to Adobe Edge Animate

A brief introduction

Hello Edge enthusiast!
Since you have purchased this publication, I can safely assume that you are eager to get to know Edge Animate from inside out and that you can’t wait to create awesome and engaging web content. That is awesome. But before we get started, I would like to take a few moments to introduce you to the underlying intention of this publication and how it works.

This publication is considered to be a guide book for working with Edge Animate. It is designed to give a profound introduction to the tool and its functionality as well as the context in which it is to be used. As such, it starts from the very beginning and with the absolute and fundamental basics. There is no previous knowledge required. If you already know a thing or two about Edge Animate, it might be useful to freshen up your knowledge. Also keep in mind, that you don’t have to read the chapters from end to end. It is no problem to jump in and out at any point.

In addition to this knowledge base, we have prepared a lot of practical examples and challenges to provide you with insights on how to actually work with the tool in real workflows. Many of the examples given are in fact derived from real life projects and related issues. This will get you up and running for the daily business.

But maybe you prefer to get your hands on right away and rather want to explore the tool on your own? No problem: We have included a whole bunch of sample projects and tutorials that just wait to be explored. We also included templates that let you build useful Animate composition for different purposes fast and easy.

And that’s not all: This publication is meant to support and assist you with future projects as well. Therefore we have included a lot of best practices and additional helpful information as well as a couple of cheat sheets to increase your performance. They also make it a lot easier to get used to the tool.

Last but not least, we also want to say Thank You! We are only able to work on such publications because of the support we get from the community. We are already working on a follow up to this edition. If you think it is worth it, you can help make it happen. Follow us @EdgeDocks to always get the latest news.

But now let's have a look at what we are going to find in this guide book and how to use it:

How to use this guide book

This first edition of Tour de Edge gives you a general and profound introduction into Edge Animate and helps you get started with your first projects. It provides you with the necessary knowledge base and also comes along with practical examples. You can work through it from end to end to get the full picture, or use it as reference book to look up specific aspects.

In case you want to start with a practical exercise right away, make sure to check out the first Workshop. Just open the provided project and follow the instructions in the manual or sit back and watch the tutorial video.

With the Recipes you have access to ready made solutions for a variety of cases that might come up or at least proove to be helpful with Edge Animate projects. For each recipes you have the corresponding Animate project and a detailed instruction in order to understand and recreate it. Apart from that, these tutorials and all the other projects, samples and templates can serve as inspiration and starting point for new projects.

In case you are yet still hungry for more: There will be a follow up edition, that will take you deeper into the secrets of Edge Animate and cover some of the more advanced topics we had to leave out for this getting started edition.

»Let us know what you think!«

There is always room for improvement.
Don't forget to give us feedback so we can make the experience even better.

Further recommendations

At the end of this introductory chapter you will find a list of helpful sources and links to websites we recommend - not only for Edge Animate but the context of modern web in general. If you want to experience the full potential of this fascinating tool, you will sooner or later be faced with the challenges of the web.

This selection of sources is far from complete, but it might give you a good starting point and set you off in the right direction. With this guide book we want to assist you throughout the first steps with Edge Animate, but at the same time encourage you to dive deeper into this fascinating world called modern web.

Enjoy!
